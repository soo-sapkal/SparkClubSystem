Faculty Coordinator Dashboard Features (Detailed)

Faculty Coordinator is the institutional oversight authority assigned to a specific club by the Super Admin.

Their role is governance, compliance, approvals, monitoring, intervention, and student welfare oversight — not day-to-day execution.
