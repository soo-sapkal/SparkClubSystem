# Club Head Guide

The Club Head dashboard is geared towards execution management.

## Key Features
* **Department Creator**: Group members into Technical, Marketing, or Sponsorship squads.
* **Event Modeler**: Pitch new events, specify targets, and assign organizers.
* **Task Board**: Assign tasks, monitor completion status, and log deadlines.
* **Sponsorship CRM**: Track conversations, expected value, and status of sponsorship pipelines.
